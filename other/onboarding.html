Are you sure you want to turn off Quick Start?

Quick Start launches BetterCanva in less then 0.2 seconds, and allows you to immiediently go back to where you left off. Disabling this will significantly reduce loading time of BetterCanva, and is only recommended if you are low on RAM at the moment.

Introduction 🖌️

We introduced BetterCanva for the 'better canva'. It bundles features that Canva should have had by default. It also fixes the key annoyences of the Canva Desktop app - slow, inconsistent, and missing features that Canva should have added to the desktop app.

We hope you enjoy it - BetterCanva is free and it always will be.

BetterDash 🚀

The personlised dashboard allows you to jump straight into your workspace.

The built in LucidSearch allows you to type anything, and find designs in your recently opened projects, or create a new design in a few words. You can also directly create a new design with the launchpad feature, and start designing in one click.

Access your themes, local upload management, and more features coming soon.

Focus Canvas 🎶

Want to get back into that locked-in 'zone'? Focus Canvas is a feature that in my opinion should have came with Canva by default.

Focus Canvas, when enabled, hides the top bar in your design, that usally shows tabs such as 'Home', 'File', 'Share', exporting options, and more. These features are still useful and used, and you can simply move your cursor to the top of the Canva tab to reshow it dynamically.

Fast-er Uploads ⬆️

Want to upload a MASSIVE file? We make that possible. Standard uploading is annoying - you have to keep the app open, is slow, and breaks often.

Local Uploads in BetterCanva fixes this. Simply drag your uploads into the BetterCanva Uploads folder, and the app will upload your files for you directly to Canva, even if the window is closed.

Access the folder manually, or see uploads, remove them, view status, and directly open the folder from the BetterDash launcher.

LucidSearch 🔍

Type anything. Search anything.

LucidSearch is an all-in-one command palette for BetterCanva - press Ctrl + Alt + Space anywhere in the app, or press the 🔎 icon in the navigation bar to search design history, or create a new design in one click.

You can also access LucidSearch directly from BetterDash.
